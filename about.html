About the Creator

Hi, I'm Lenchell Grace O. Borda! I created Daily Whispers because I wanted a quiet, gentle space where I could express myself freely. Some memories deserve a place to stay, even if they're small.

  • Those who want to keep memories safely
  • Anyone who wants a peacful place to write
